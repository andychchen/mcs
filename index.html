宗旨
本校創校目的在傳延中華文化，創立的宗旨為提供在密西根州、大底特律地區的華人及非華人家庭孩子學習中國語言及文化的場所。本校的主要活動除了上課之外，還包括新年慶祝晚會、專題演講、識字比賽及演講比賽等活動。本校亦經常參加社區活動，促進與非華人社區的文化交流和友好關係。


本校特點
1. 本校目前借用 Madonna University 的校舍，於每星期六下午1:30-4:30 PM上課三小時，前兩小時為中文課，後一小時為課外活動。
2. 本校使用流傳全新版華語, 生活華語以及美洲華語等中文課本為主，輔以課外補充教材。
3. 特別開設 CFL (Chinese as Foreign Language) 班，提供不會講中文的學生及非華人家庭的孩子學習中國語文及文化的機會 。
4. 課外活動，由專業人才授課，開設的課程包括舞獅、Lego Robotics、跆拳道、舞蹈、兒童潛能班、成人太極拳、Chinese Communication Group。
5. 本校為向密西根州政府正式登記的非營利組織，不附屬於任何團體，除教師以外，所有職員均為義務工作人員。

Michigan Chinese School (MCS) provides support to the education of the Chinese language and culture with a racially non discriminatory policy towards it's students. The School is an equal opportunity employer with a policy of zero tolerance against discrimination on the basis of race, age, and sexual orientation with respect to admissions, use of facilities, faculty and scholarship.

MCS is a non-profit organization registered with 501(c)(3) status, with the main objectives of teaching Chinese language, promoting Chinese culture and providing these related services to the general public.

MCS was established September 1998. Currently, there are 9 classes with different ages: preschool, and grades 1-10 as well as CFL (Chinese as a Foreign Language).

Each school year starts after Labor Day and ends mid-June totaling 32 school weeks. MCS meets Saturday's from 1:30 to 3:30 PM. Classes are conducted in Mandarin and students learn traditional Chinese characters as well as phonetic symbols and the Pin-Yin system. Optional extracurricular activities are provided after class. Activities vary but often include Lego-Robotics, Tai-Kwon-Do, Dance, Arts, Choir, Lion Dance Team and more.

MCS yearly holds a speech competition, reading month, and word recognition contest. Through these events, MCS hopes to promote students' interest in learning Chinese as well as their appreciation of the Chinese Culture. MCS also actively encourages its students to participate in major community events in the school's vicinity.

MCS not only stresses Chinese Language instruction, but it gives special emphasis to spreading Chinese culture as well. It asserts the importance of both the student's academic achievement and their mental and physical well being. MCS provides local Chinese descendants with one of the best environments to learn Chinese culture, arts, and language. 收費 Tuition: $170 per student per semester, $135 the 3rd student and up in the same family.
