FW: Referrals Needed- Sr Project Engineer CAE Altair Irvine CA

 

HI Everyone

 

We currently have an immediate need in the Irvine CA office, if you are interested or know of anyone that would be interested and qualified please send me their resume. Thanks Shannon

 

Senior Project Engineer CAE at our Altair ProductDesign's consulting engineering group in Irvine, CA

 

This role will be part of Altair’s ProductDesign engineering practice and will be have exposure to a wide variety of engineering projects and client verticals. The ideal candidate will thrive on variety and will be energized by applying their skills across a wide variety of the engineering domains that they already are familiar with as well as learning some of the others that Altair ProductDesign serves including:

 

  • Military armor (composite and metallic) and vehicle design
  • Consumer electronics including video game consoles and mobile phone handsets
  • Aerospace – missiles, aircraft, spacecraft, etc.
  • Biomed
  • Heavy and light vehicles

 

In addition to the skills listed below, the candidate will have the opportunity to expand their skills in a number of areas including:

 

  • Topology optimization
  • Blast / ballistics
  • Crash / drop testing
  • Packaging optimization
  • Biomedical design

 

Education: Bachelors in Mechanical/Aerospace, MS Preferred.

 

Experience/Responsibilities:

 

  • 8 to 10 years in performing stress hand calculations, FEA simulations, Static, Dynamic, NVH, and Optimization simulations.  Composite experience a plus.
  • Software Expertise: HyperMesh, HyperView, Radioss Explicit, Radioss Implicit, Optistruct Optimization, LS-Dyna, Abaqus Standard/Explicit, MS Office.
  • Experience in performing FE analysis in blast and ballistics a plus.
  • Excellent fundamental engineering, written and communication skills
  • Able to write and review technical FEA reports

 

The ideal candidate will work and interface directly with our clients

  • Develop, refine, continue and lead FEA projects to support our clients to the highest level
  • Manage, mentor and guide junior engineers.
  • Candidate may be required to be onsite at customer location for limited durations (in state or out of state) on certain project engagements
  • Provide specialized trainings to team engineers
  • Lead FEA projects from proposal writing to project completion

 

Location: Irvine CA.

 

Travel: 30%

 

Status: Must be a US Citizen
