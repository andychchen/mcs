Concert of Taiwanese Music at Farmington Community Library, Feb. 7, 2:30pm

From: Tzy-Wen Gong

Hello, everyone.

On behalf of Michigan Taiwanese American Organization (MiTAI), I would like to invite you to a concert of Taiwanese music organized and hosted by Farmington Musicale, cosponsored by our organization. This one-hour concert on Feb. 7th (Sunday) will start at 2:30pm, Farmington Community Library (32737 W Twelve Mile Rd. Farmington Hills MI. 248.563.8300).

Performers in this program include some participants of the 7th TMF as well as honor guesses of previous years. This list include (but not in the order of performance):
1. Ziqing Sunny Huang (A Sketch of Rainy Harbor by Shui-Long Ma)
2. Jessica Xu (Dragon Dance by Shui-Long Ma)
3. Heritage String Quartet (Dance for Moon Watching, arranged by Diau-Hua Lin)
4. Grace Mieczkowski (Meditation by Huang Huang)
5. Anna Mieczkowski (Amei Aborigines’ Capriccios by Shu Si Chen)
6. Catherine and Christopher Li, cello duet (Wish for Your Early Return, Spring All Over the Mountain, White Peony, arranged by Chin-Ju Shih)
7. Ming-Hsiu Yen (Two Old Postcards from Formosa for solo piano by Ming-Hsiu Yen)
8. Angela Wu, D.M.A. (Fantasy by Chin-Ju Shih)

It sure will be a wonderful concert of high caliber. What a great way to celebrate the upcoming Lunar New Year! A small reception will follow the music program. The concert will be videotaped for local cable TV broadcasting to Farmington, Farmington Hills, and Novi areas. Please help spreading the news and extend invitation to family and friends. Look forward to seeing you at the concert.

Tzywen Gong
President
Michigan Taiwanese American Organization
