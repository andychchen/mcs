      您好，我的名字叫李君梅。
我在 2008 年的十二月拿到密西根小學教師執照，但是我對教育的熱愛卻早在多年以前。我出生於台灣， 在台完成了專科教育，從事教兒童英語的工作。雖然學生學習的成果、以及對我教學的喜愛，給我很大的肯定。但是，我想進一步進修，希望我的教學不單單能幫助 程度好的同學，也能讓學習上有困難的孩子獲益。於是我來到美國進修教育，希望能從美式教育中，學習到在中國傳統教育的理念及方法中所欠缺的部份。
1999 年底，我順利的完成教育博士學位，最後決定留在家裏照顧剛出生的孩子。在接下來的十年中，我除了照顧自己的孩子，也在教會教導青年 (青少年) 主日學。2005年開始，我在密西根中文學校教高中　/ 中學班的中文，我也在東密西根大學修課拿教育執照。上課中所學習到的幫助我應用在中文教學中，從反思教學的成效中，我進一步調整我對學生學習和需要的認 識，用更適當的教學策略來幫助孩子來學習。
對我而言，中文教學的重點不單是語言使用上量的增加，幫助學生更深一層地了解中國固有的文化資產也是我上課的重點。藉由教材所提供的豊富資料，讓學生從文 學重點中學習閱讀和寫作的技巧。再從老師和同學的對話中，比較中西思想的異同，而進一步看到中國傳統思想的精髓。
教導孩童是一項很有意義的工作。雖然在教學中會遇到挑戰，但是當我看見帶給學生正面影嚮時所給我的滿足卻是更深的。在此，盼望能有機會可以更進一步的和您 分享我對教育的想法和熱愛。謝謝。

Mei’s philosophy of Teaching
I believe teaching and learning are the key elements that enrich all human experiences. When teaching builds upon meaning and connections, it creates a powerful impact on the further growth of individuals. However, if teachers stop learning, his/her ability to reach out to learners will eventually die off.
All children can learn. It is the responsibility of a teacher to provide a learning environment conducive to learning. Children have a natural curiosity to learn and tendency to respond. They are always busy at finding something to occupy themselves. Learning occurs as a result of children responding to a well-designed instruction. It is my job as a teacher to present instruction and materials that will capture the attention of a child and develop his thirst of learning.
I believe teaching and learning happen when a child is given opportunity to learn and function at his own pace. Therefore, it is my job as a teacher to continuously find ways to teach each individual child. Sometimes, it means that I need to create a structure where I have an opportunity to facilitate the learning of each child while others children engage in learning at his/her own level.  Children come to school with different backgrounds and interests. They are always at different levels with their learning. Without feeling capable, a child becomes hopeless and gives up on learning. They need to feel safe and good about themselves as well as the learning in order to work through a challenge. My goal as a teacher is to help each child make progress and engage in an ongoing learning process. 
Children are like the flowers of a garden. When seeds are provided with a right condition, they will sprout and grow. Teacher’s job is like the gardener who works hard to provide all the necessary ingredients and remove the weeds whenever necessary. A wise gardener continues to apply the best knowledge to care, carefully observe the growing throughout the entire process and expect the flower to grow and blossom. Likewise, I as a teacher am committed in this wonderful growing process of children until they grow and succeed.
