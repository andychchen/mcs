Dear ACA members and friends,

We are glad to announce the launch of a new youth training program here at the Chinese Community Center!

Digital Connectors is a program held by Comcast every year in which kids ages 14-17 have the chance to participate. We are now recruiting 20 students to participate in this year-long course.  In the program, students have the chance to learn how to use computers, applications and the internet. They will also learn on how to be a better leader in their community, how to individually grow into strong and stable adults, have a better understanding on the financial aspect throughout their lives, understand mass media and its basic concepts, and even how to build greener communities. 

 

Throughout this course, they will be committing 156 hours of class time which will be split up into two classes a week (Sunday 2-6pm & Thursday 4-6pm). They will also be providing a minimum of 50 hours of community service. As a result of their commitment, not only will they have a chance to be Cisco certified they will be guaranteed a netbook & video camera as long as they complete the required hours. 

 

Attached please see the program flyer and application form.  If you know anyone who is interested and would like to apply, please fill out the application form and email it to us by Friday, September 21 and he/she will be contacted with more details.
