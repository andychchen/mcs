SAT Writing Course
Fall 2009
Michigan Chinese School

Purpose:
The SAT Writing Course is designed to familiarize students with the format of the writing section and prepare them to succeed in that specific section of the College Board’s SAT exam.  Topics that will be covered include: writing an structured and well-organized essay, effectively managing time restraints, improving sentences, identifying sentence errors, and improving paragraph questions.

By the end of the course, students should have the necessary test strategies and knowledgebase to succeed on the SAT Writing Section.
