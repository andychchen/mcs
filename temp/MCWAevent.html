CAPA (Council of Asian Pacific Americans) leadership Summit event

An invitation of an upcoming CAPA (Council of Asian Pacific Americans) leadership Summit event to be held in Novi on Oct. 24.
The program agenda and the registration process is shown below. There will be several speakers address issues on health, federal grant writing and education/mentoring. MCWA is participating in the planning commitee as well as the Summit event. Hope you may find?benefit in your organization and participate in this event. If possible, you may forward to those who may be interested.
The event consists of several Asian organizations to organize the workshop forums. The even also serve as an opportunity to networking with the other organizations. Your are cordially invited to attend the event. Below is the updated program agenda. This is also a reminder that the early registration deadline is Oct. 20 with $20.00 registration fee.
